# C36 conservative barycentric A0 terminal

C36 validated the common-plus-contrastive decomposition but falsified its
additional soft trust coefficient. Fifteen paired GPU fits passed 35 of 36
label-free A0 checks. The candidate-axis barycenter was preserved to numerical
precision, exact abstention and mixed admission replicated on a new cohort, no
candidate write reversed the shared tangent direction, and all controls,
fallbacks, corruption, determinism, and capacity checks behaved as registered.

The only failure was nearest-neighbor activity: the primary and the exact
unbounded barycentric reduction differed in 133/600 complete rankings but only
2/600 top-10 sets. This is not evidence that the threshold was too strict. It
means the always-on coefficient `||g||/(||g||+max||delta||)` paid almost no
mechanism rent at top rank. C36 stopped before labels, so there is no utility
result to reinterpret.

The surviving sub-hypothesis is simpler: active-set barycentric conservation
around a shared global write. It must become a new candidate with the untouched
C36 delayed-B cohort, fresh seeds, and global/uncentered/relative controls. The
failed soft shrinkage must not be included or tuned. C36-A may be used only for
label-free formulation diagnostics and never for the successor's gate.

Curated report: `reports/pps_c36_train_gate.json`. Internal-A labels,
delayed-B, escrow, dev, and test remain closed.

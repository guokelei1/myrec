## Final evidence ledger

### Executed scientific negative results

- **C02 CHHT:** after the one neutral repair, all five variants completed two
  epochs.  CHHT had zero non-repeat gain and zero margin over its best control;
  four corruption norm ratios were approximately one.  Every history-present
  core hit radius `0.35`, the score residual became an almost common `+1.5`,
  only 9/3,000 requests changed order under the shared tie-break and none
  changed top-10 membership.
- **C04:** the frozen query/null anchor failed before valid dev admission;
  paired-prefix transfer was negative and corruption-insensitive within that
  primitive's admissible evidence.
- **C05:** ordinary target attention produced a candidate-common `+1`
  translation and zero ranking change.
- **C06:** a numerically valid, zero-sum local-Hodge write was too small to be
  load-bearing: 0/1,200 order and top-10 changes at label-free A0.
- **C07--C10:** their frozen learned synthetic gates failed the registered
  nearest-control, repeat, non-repeat or corruption criteria.  C10's absolute
  generator difficulty has a recorded shortcut caveat, but its approximately
  `-0.05` result versus same-data centered attention remains informative.

### Closed without a ranking-mechanism claim

- **C01:** wrong D2p anchor invalidates its training and internal comparisons.

  completion of the frozen attempt; later evidence also closes its global

- **C11--C16:** each was stopped before model/data/GPU outcome because its

## What the completed exploration establishes

It does **not** establish that history contains no cross-item signal or that no
future Transformer can use it.  It establishes a tighter architectural
boundary:

1. a history write must be candidate-relative in its final score effect, not
   merely candidate-conditioned inside the network;
2. it needs a load-bearing lower bound as well as a safety upper bound;
3. evidence direction must be ranking-aligned and corruption-specific before
   its norm, support mass or uncertainty can be trusted;
4. exact recurrence needs a monotone final-ranking contract separate from
   speculative transfer;
5. gates, NULL mass, RMS/whitening, pair MLPs, likelihood ratios, gradients,
   Hessians and energy language are not innovations when they reduce to known
   attention or message-passing operators; and
6. a successor requires a new motivation-derived structural law and a fresh
   evidence budget.  It cannot be assembled from the closed C01--C16 parts.

# C80 preimplementation review

Decision: `authorize_label_free_materialization_then_fresh_real_gate`.

C80 is not an untested composition: its exact discrete `triadic_set` graph was
the strongest C78 control and passed clean, shuffled, wrong, query-mask,
no-history, and repeat behavior in every seed.  The remaining question is
whether pretrained WordPiece anchors instantiate the same boundary on real
product text and pay rent over ordinary full-token BGE.

The 365-request role is small but uniquely clean: every user is disjoint from
fit and no request was used by the 1,200 HSO reserve.  It is sufficient for a
terminal train-internal mechanism decision, not a final test claim.  Dev/test
remain unavailable unless all C80 gates pass and a separate paper protocol is
approved.

C80 is the final architecture update.  Outcome-dependent tuning is prohibited;
the retrospective follows either decision.

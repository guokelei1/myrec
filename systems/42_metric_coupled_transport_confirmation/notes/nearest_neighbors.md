# C42 nearest-neighbor boundary

C42 inherits C40/C41's literature audit. QKV projection sharing, identity
values, metric learning, residual adapters, multi-head attention, and Hopfield
retrieval are not claimed as new. Its only purpose is to determine whether the
full ranker-side metric loop replicates over C38 and matched controls on a
second untouched Amazon cohort. A pass triggers, but does not replace, a
separate novelty review and cross-dataset confirmation.

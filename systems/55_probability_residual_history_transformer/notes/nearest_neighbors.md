# C55 claim boundary

C55 changes the supervised target, not the architecture fingerprint.  Its
components have direct neighbors in residual learning, boosting, listwise
ranking, and teacher-error modeling.  The probability residual `y-p_base` is
the negative softmax-cross-entropy logit gradient.  C28 already used
standardized base margins, while C54 supplies the history-only value path.

Therefore C55 cannot be the paper innovation regardless of outcome.  Its only
purpose is to decide whether a next LM-internal architecture has a stable,
history-specific strong-base error signal to learn.
